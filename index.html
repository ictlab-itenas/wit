About Women In Tech

Women In Tech is a community dedicated to supporting and empowering women in the technology sector. We believe in creating an inclusive environment where women can thrive, learn, and lead in tech.

Our mission is to bridge the gender gap in technology by providing resources, mentorship, and opportunities for women to excel in their tech careers.
